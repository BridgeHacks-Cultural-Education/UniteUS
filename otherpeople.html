A Person a Day

Each day, a new person--out of the thousands who have positively impacted the United States--is displayed here on the UniteUS platform.

The celebrated individuals that are displayed come from a variety of races, gender identities, ethnicities, sexual orientations, religious affiliations, and other various backgrounds in order to showcase the incredible accomplishments and beautiful diversity found within the country.
